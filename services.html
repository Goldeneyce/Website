Comprehensive Lift Services

From consultation to maintenance, we provide end-to-end solutions for all your vertical transportation needs.

Our Service Offerings

Product Distribution

As a licensed distributor of Japan Fuji and Shanghai Mitsubishi elevators, we provide access to world-class vertical transportation solutions. Our product portfolio includes:

  • Passenger elevators for commercial and residential use
  • Freight and service elevators for industrial applications
  • Accessibility solutions including platform lifts and stairlifts
  • Specialized lifts for unique architectural requirements

We work closely with clients to select the right product for their specific needs, budget, and architectural constraints.

Professional Installation

Our certified installation team ensures that your lift system is installed correctly, safely, and in compliance with all local regulations. Our installation services include:

  • Site assessment and preparation guidance
  • Precise installation by factory-trained technicians
  • Comprehensive testing and commissioning
  • Final inspection and certification
  • Handover and user training

We pride ourselves on minimal disruption to your operations during installation, with clear timelines and regular progress updates.

Maintenance & Repair

Regular maintenance is essential for the safety, reliability, and longevity of your lift system. We offer flexible maintenance plans:

  • Basic Care: Scheduled inspections and lubrication
  • Comprehensive Care: Full maintenance including parts replacement
  • Platinum Care: 24/7 priority response with full coverage

Our emergency repair service is available 24/7 to minimize downtime and ensure passenger safety.

Modernization & Upgrades

Extend the life of your existing elevator system with our modernization services. We can help you:

  • Improve safety features to meet current standards
  • Enhance energy efficiency with modern drive systems
  • Update aesthetics with new cabin interiors and doors
  • Upgrade control systems for smoother operation
  • Increase capacity and speed where possible

Modernization is often more cost-effective than complete replacement and can significantly improve performance.

Consultation & Design

Our experts provide comprehensive consultation services to help you make informed decisions about your vertical transportation needs:

  • Space planning and traffic analysis
  • Specification development
  • Regulatory compliance guidance
  • Budget planning and life-cycle cost analysis
  • Integration with building management systems

We work with architects, builders, and property owners from the earliest planning stages to ensure optimal results.
